Frequently Asked Questions

Questions people always ask about the Needy Africa Organisation.

1. Where are Needy Africa offices located?

Masaka Uganda

2. What does Needy Africa do?

Needy bAfrica raises funds through various means, including donations from individuals, grants from governments or private foundations, fundraising events, sponsorships from businesses, and membership fees. It also generates income through the sale of products or services related to their mission.

3. What are the benefits of donating to a non-profit?

Donating to a non-profit can provide several benefits:
Personal Satisfaction: Knowing that your contribution supports a cause you care about can be very rewarding.
Tax Deductions: In many countries, donations to registered non-profits are tax-deductible.
Community Impact: Your donation helps the non-profit achieve its mission and create positive changes in the community.
